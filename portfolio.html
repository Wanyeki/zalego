Recruitment
Acquire the right people that best fit the needs of your smaller business, and onboard them quickly and efficiently. Our solution helps simplify your recruiting and onboarding processes and lay the foundation for employee success, starting before day one. Through the system HR can post a job opening which will go internally to the staff members and can then be advertised externally if the person being searched for is not already a staff members. When posting a job, HR has an option to choose whether she/he would like to have applicants to take a test during the application process. This particular feature helps the HR to filter out candidates who are not eligible for the Job posting. All applicants who apply for the Jobs are listed on the system allowing the HR to shortlist qualified candidates. Candidates that qualify for the interview receive an email communication notifying them of set date for the interview. Once a candidate passes the interview, the system allows HR to promote candidates who passed the interview to the next positions whether intern, attaché, or staff on contract or permanent or based on your preferred and set description. The process of promoting enables the HR not to do double data entry when a particular candidate is confirmed.

SMS Solutions

Whether passing a message to customers or marketing to potential clients, our bulk messaging service can reach out to millions worldwide using our powerful and easy to use messaging platform that has limitless options. No programming skills are required and it accommodates any organization size whether a personal business or a large corporate. Some of its features include;

SMS (Short Messaging Service) This is a fast and simple to use channel for text messages that Doesn’t require internet connectivity.

Features:

  • Intelligent Routing – Our powerful engine finds the shortest message for every message.
  • Numbers – Local long number or Short Code available in more than 45 countries.
  • Long Messages in any Country – One can send messages longer than 160 characters without additional setup and our platform will ensure it displays as you intended. Messages can be in any language.
  • 2-Way Communication– Receive reply messages from your customers and start a conversation.
  • Real-time Integrations– Integrate your system with us and send messages seamlessly from your system.
  • Personalization – Personalize each message with as many elements as you need.

VOICEReach more customers worldwide with crystal – clear voice communications. Our platform supports both outbound and inbound voice calls over API, SIP integration or on our platform. One can combine voice with other channels like SMS, email or chat apps.

Features:

  • Text - to - speech (TTS) - Converts written text into an audio file that can be played to your customers over the phone.
  • Pre-recorded Audio Files – Play a pre-recorded audio to your customers that can be sent via call.
  • Interactive Voice Response (IVR) – Automates voice call processes for your business. Provides you with a 2-way communication channel allowing you to interact with your customers on call.
  • Click to Call – helps you empower your website with voice capability using our Click-to-call API.

E-MAIL Manage and create marketing e-mails, e-newsletters, or customer service replies with a single, integrated platform. One can easily manage contacts and segments in the People module. You can also create and manage email body templates with the Drag and Drop Design Editor or HTML Code Editor.

Features:

  • Rich Design Editor - Create rich email messages with the drag-and-drop email builder. Add buttons, images, HTML, and content blocks; adjust layout and styles to make your email look great on any device.
  • Templates – Build a template once and use it as many times as you need to save time and remain consistent. Build custom, rich templates using the Email design editor or HTML code editor and send them from our API or Portal.
  • Personalization – Personalize all your emails for each customer. First name, address, subscription, or any other custom value is supported.
  • Tracking – Collect service quality feedback and customer engagement insights by tracking email delivery, bounce rates, spam reports, link clicks, opens, and unsubscribes.
